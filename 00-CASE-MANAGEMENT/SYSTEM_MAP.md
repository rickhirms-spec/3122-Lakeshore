# 3122 Lakeshore System Map

## Purpose
This repository serves as the central evidence, correspondence, governance, and project record for matters relating to 3122 Lakeshore and the condominium corporation.

## Repository Structure

### 00-CASE-MANAGEMENT
Master indexes, timelines, issue logs, and AI navigation documents.

### 01-BOARD-CORRESPONDENCE
Emails and written communications with property management and the Board.

### 02-GOVERNANCE
Condo Act references, bylaws, rules, policies, owner requisitions, and meeting materials.

### 03-CONTRACTOR-WORKMANSHIP
Construction deficiencies, inspections, contractor communications, and remediation requests.

### 04-PAVING-STONES
Paving stone replacement requests, approvals, denials, photos, and correspondence.

### 05-PATIO-FENCING
Fence removal, reinstallation, damage concerns, and related evidence.

### 06-FLASHING-WATERPROOFING
Flashing, sealant, drainage, leaks, threshold issues, and weatherproofing concerns.

### 07-EVIDENCE
Photos, videos, consultant opinions, inspection notes, and supporting documents.

### 08-LEGAL-RESEARCH
CAT materials, Condo Act references, legal analysis, and precedent research.

### 09-TIMELINES
Chronological records of events, communications, and decisions.

### 10-OWNER-MEETING-REQUISITION
Owner meeting requests, signatures, notices, and supporting materials.

## AI Retrieval Notes
Start with SYSTEM_MAP.md, CASE_INDEX.md, and TIMELINE.md when researching any issue.
