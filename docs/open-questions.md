# Open Questions for Management / Board

## Scope of Work

1. What is the specific scope of work applicable to Unit 29?
2. Were balcony/terrace pavers included for replacement, repair, restoration, reuse, or exclusion?
3. Was fencing included?
4. Were drainage components included?
5. Was the threshold area included?
6. Were sidewalls, coping, membrane, bedding, leveling, or related transition areas included?

## Paver Replacement / Reuse

1. What criteria were used to determine which pavers/stones were replaced versus reused?
2. Who made the replacement/reuse decisions?
3. Were decisions based on engineering, contractor discretion, Board direction, budget, condition, appearance, safety, water management, or availability of materials?
4. Were criteria applied consistently across units?
5. Why did some damaged/chipped pavers appear to be changed after concerns were raised?

## Owner-Funded Replacement Request

1. Has the Board reviewed the owner-funded paver replacement request?
2. Has the request been approved, denied, or deferred?
3. If denied, what reason and criteria were relied upon?
4. If deferred, when will the Board respond?
5. Would owner-funded replacement be allowed if coordinated through management/contractor rather than direct owner instruction?

## Workmanship / Inspection

1. Has management inspected water pooling near the threshold?
2. Has the Board or engineer reviewed the drainage condition?
3. Is one drainage opening sufficient for the current configuration?
4. Has the threshold gap/sealant condition been reviewed?
5. Will deficiencies be corrected before final project closeout?

## Communication / Allegations

1. What exactly is alleged regarding entry into restricted areas?
2. What evidence supports the allegation?
3. Did management review call attempts and customer service instructions?
4. Was a manager or authorized representative on site and reachable?
5. Will the Board review the communication breakdown before any chargeback or warning?
