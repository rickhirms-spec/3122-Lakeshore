# Vendor / Contractor Communication Protocol

## Source

June 2025 notice: Reminder — Communication Protocol with Trades and Service Providers.

## Management Position in Notice

Residents and owners are not permitted to provide direction to or make individual requests of trades or service providers working on site.

Trades are contracted by the Corporation and are to follow instructions from management or authorized Board representatives.

Residents with concerns, questions, or observations regarding work should not approach workers directly and should direct inquiries to the Property Manager.

## Relevance to Current Dispute

The notice supports the idea that owners should not direct trades. However, it also assumes management is available to receive concerns/questions/observations.

If management was unreachable, and no authorized representative was available on site, the issue becomes a communication breakdown rather than simply an owner refusing to follow protocol.

## Suggested Framing

For the record, we understand residents are not to direct trades or contractors. Our concern is that the Corporation's own communication protocol directs owners to management for concerns, questions, or observations, but no manager or authorized representative was available or reachable when clarification was required regarding work affecting our unit.
