## Key Issues

1. The owner initially sought core records by email and linked the request to concerns about special assessments.
2. Management's position was that the CAO-prescribed records request process had to be followed.
3. Counsel responded to the owner and provided CAO guidance materials.
4. The owner then submitted a CAO Request for Records form to counsel.
5. Counsel later responded that her office was not the address for service and that the request was not submitted in accordance with the Act.
6. John McKenzie then took over the procedural correction and asked that the requester date be updated and the form resubmitted.
7. John followed up twice, on Dec. 30, 2025 and Jan. 7, 2026, stating the request was not yet valid/submitted.
