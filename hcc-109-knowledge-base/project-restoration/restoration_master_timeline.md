## Key Findings

1. Repair/restoration history starts at least by 2023 with steel beam repair updates and garage access inspections.
2. 2024 notices show continued engineering involvement and garage beam repair communications under MRCM management.
3. By July 15, 2025, the Board linked the reserve fund work to Synergy Partners, Edison Engineering, Avangarde Restoration Corp., and urgent unit classification.
4. Unit 29 was specifically included in the urgent reserve fund work group and appears to have been assigned to the 2026 phase.
5. The Board stated that first stage reserve fund work included terrace waterproofing and masonry/lintel repairs.
6. The Board also stated that retaining walls, walkways, and shingles were being assessed/planned as additional work.
